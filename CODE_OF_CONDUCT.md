# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple code of conduct to ensure a respectful environment.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and kind
- No harassment or discrimination
- No hate speech or toxic behavior
- Help others learn

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting
- Sharing illegal or harmful content

## Enforcement
Maintainers have the right to remove any content or contributor that violates this code.
